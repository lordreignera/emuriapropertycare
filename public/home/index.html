Welcome to EMURIA Property Care

Regenerative Property Care Solutions

How EMURIA Property Care Works

We believe in a diagnostic approach to property care - like a doctor prescribing care based on real data, not selling packages. Our intelligent system calculates the right solution for your specific property needs.

1. Register FREE

Create your account and add your property details. No payment required, no credit card needed.

2. Professional Inspection

Our expert inspector conducts a comprehensive property assessment to understand your unique needs.

3. Custom Plan Created

Our AI-powered system analyzes 7 key factors and generates a personalized care plan just for you.

4. Choose Your Model

Pay-as-you-go for flexibility, or subscribe monthly/annually for savings. You decide what works best.
