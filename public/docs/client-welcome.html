Thank you for registering with EMURIA Regenerative Property Care. We are delighted to welcome you to our platform and look forward to partnering with you to restore, protect, and maintain your property to the highest standard.

Our approach is built on a single principle: proactive, science-based property care that prevents deterioration before it becomes costly. Through our Property Health Assessment Report (PHAR) process, we assess every critical system of your property and build a personalised regenerative care programme tailored specifically to your needs.

⚡ Your Next Steps — What Happens From Here

  • 1
    Register Your PropertyLogin and go to Properties → Add Property. Enter your property details and any known issues. This takes about 5 minutes.
  • 2
    Pay the Inspection Fee — $299 USDGo to Services → Inspections and click "Pay Inspection Fee". This covers the full on-site PHAR assessment by our team.
  • 3
    Await Your Scheduled VisitWe will contact you to confirm the inspection date. Our inspector or project manager will visit your property and carry out the assessment.
  • 4
    Review Your QuotationAfter the visit, we share a detailed quotation listing all findings and recommended remediation. You select which findings to address.
  • 5
    Sign the Agreement & Pay 50% DepositOnce you agree on scope and pricing, sign the service agreement digitally and pay 50% to kick off your project.
